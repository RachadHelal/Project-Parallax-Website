Welcome!

Once upon a time on a sunny beautiful day, there was Labib, sitting on a bench contemplating life.
Labib is very lost in life and is wondering what he could study in order to become a very successful man with a reputable profession.
Labib suddenly realizes that software engineers are very highly in demand now adays and it Is a very respectable job that pays extremely well. He therefore decides to enroll in a 14 week full stack web development bootcamp.
